PEEP

by Yeqian Li

The inspiration for this project came from a poster on Weibo (a popular social network in China) in which women living alone discussed their experiences of being peeped. It reminded me that women have always been troubled by peeping in hotels, subways, toilets, and even in their own homes. Therefore, I made a device with four typical scenes of women being peeped, showing the harms. Moreover, the opening of the gift packaging also makes the audience becomes a peeper.

Research

I searched for four scenes where women are easy to be peeped, and researched the peeping ways and medium, related news, harm to women and so on.

Making Process

Characters: Clay/Other props: Cardboard, clay and paper etc./Coloring material: Watercolor, acrylic
